Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Sudoku Showdown is a multiplayer sudoku game. We collect the minimum data necessary to make multiplayer work — and nothing more.

What we collect

Data Purpose Storage
Username
e.g. baller#1234, auto-generated		 Identifies you in multiplayer rooms and friend requests. Never linked to your real name or Apple ID. On-device
Routing key
Internal, never shown to you		 Used by our server to route multiplayer sessions. Never shared with other players. Our server
Friend request state Brokered through our server so two devices can exchange invitations. Your friends list is stored on your device only. On-device
Game results Stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your puzzle history, scores, or statistics. On-device

What we do not collect

  • Name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment or financial information
  • Analytics or crash reporting SDKs

Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The multiplayer server is operated by us solely to facilitate game sessions.

Data retention

Your username and routing key are retained on our server for as long as you use the app’s multiplayer features. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us — we will remove your record within 30 days.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
