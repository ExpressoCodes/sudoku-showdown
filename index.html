Privacy policy Sudoku Showdown Last updated: June 17, 2025 Sudoku Showdown ("the app") is a multiplayer sudoku game for iOS. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is stored. We collect the minimum necessary to make multiplayer work and nothing more. What we collect Data Purpose Auto-generated username (e.g. baller#1234) Identifies you in multiplayer rooms and friend requests. Generated randomly on your device — never linked to your real name or Apple ID. Internal routing key A hidden identifier used by our server to route multiplayer sessions. Never shown to you or other players. Friend request state Pending friend requests are brokered through our server so two devices can exchange invitations. Your saved friends list is stored on your device only and never uploaded. Game results Stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your puzzle history, scores, or statistics to any server. What we do not collect No name, email address, or Apple ID No location data No device identifiers or advertising IDs No payment or financial information No analytics or crash reporting SDKs Third parties We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The multiplayer server is operated by us solely to facilitate game sessions. Data retention Your username and routing key are retained on our server for as long as you use the app's multiplayer features. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us — we will remove your record within 30 days. Children The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, contact us and we will delete it promptly. Contact Questions about this policy? Email us at support@localjams.live
